# Oțios

- generate a list of the *least used* or forgotten words from a corpus of text. I'm thinking of comparing the official dictionaries (that include archaisms) to a corpus of text, and generate a list of words found in the dictionary but not found or seldom found in the corpus.
- identify linguistic "dark matter" - words that exist in our dictionaries but have fallen out of active use.
